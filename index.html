FAQ

Common questions

How much does a custom neon sign cost?

Most custom text signs land between $250 and $600 depending on size and complexity. Logo signs and large business signage are quoted individually. Your quote is fixed and free. You only pay once you've approved the design.

How long does delivery take to Perth?

Production takes 7-10 days, then express shipping to Perth metro typically takes 3-5 business days. Regional WA can add a couple of days. Need it for a date? Tell us in the quote form and we'll confirm before you commit.

Are LED neon signs safe and energy efficient?

Yes. We use LED neon flex, not glass tubes. It runs cool to the touch, uses a fraction of the power of traditional neon, is safe around kids and pets, and plugs into a standard power point.

Can you make my business logo as a sign?

Absolutely. Send us your logo file and we'll mock it up as neon flex, or as neon on a UV-printed backboard for full-colour logos. This is one of our most popular options for Perth businesses.

What's included with my sign?

Every sign comes with a power adapter, dimmer with remote, mounting kit, and a 12-month warranty covering any electrical faults.
